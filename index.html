About Us

Our Story

Since 1985, Michyrite Bites has been serving authentic Nigerian cuisine with passion and dedication. Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing the true taste of Nigeria to your table.

We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally when possible, and imported from overseas to ensure authenticity. Our chefs prepare each dish with love and attention to detail, just like Nonna used to make.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a meal with loved ones, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order through Telegram? +

Simply click the "Order Now via Telegram" button, and our bot will guide you through the menu. You can browse dishes, customize your order, and complete payment securely through the bot.

Do you offer delivery? +

Yes! We offer delivery within a 5-mile radius. Delivery fee is N5, and orders over N50 get free delivery. You can track your order in real-time through our Telegram bot.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods through our secure Telegram bot payment system.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please mention your dietary requirements when ordering through our Telegram bot, and we'll accommodate your needs.

Can I make reservations for dine-in? +

Yes! You can make reservations by contacting us via phone or through our Telegram bot. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and special occasions.
